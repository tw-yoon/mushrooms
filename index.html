Mushrooms in Mushroom Kingdom

Super Mushroom

This is edible. You can eat this! However, as a side effect, you will grow bigger.

Poison Mushroom

This is not edible. You will get sick! Please avoid eating this mushroom.

1-Up Mushroom

This is edible. You can eat this! When consumed, you can die one time for free!

Mini Mushroom

This is edible. You can eat this! However, as a side effect, you will become really small.

Spring Mushroom

This is edible. You can eat this! However, as a side effect, you will become a spring.

Boo Mushroom

This is edible. You can eat this! However, as a side effect, you will become a ghost.

Super Star

This is edible(?). You will become invinsible! Lets GOOOO!!!
